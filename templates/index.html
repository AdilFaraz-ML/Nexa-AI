Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IUB and Nexa AI. Can't find an answer? Chat with the bot above.

What is Nexa AI? +
Nexa AI is the official AI-powered chatbot of Islamia University of Bahawalpur (IUB). It answers queries about transport, departments, contacts, admissions, and more.
How do I use the chatbot? +
Simply type your question in the chat input and press Enter or click the send button. The bot will reply instantly.
What are the university transport timings? +
Afternoon transport services start from 12:00 PM onwards on all major university routes, effective from 03 December 2025.
How can I contact IUB administration? +
You can contact IUB at +92-62-9250235 or visit the main campus at Bahawalpur. The chatbot can also direct you to the relevant department.
Is admissions information available on Nexa AI? +
Yes! Ask the chatbot about current admissions, eligibility criteria, and deadlines. You can also visit the IUB official website for the latest notifications.
What departments does IUB have? +
IUB has departments covering Sciences, Arts, Engineering, Commerce, Islamic Studies, Law, and more. Ask the chatbot for contact details of any specific department.
Can Nexa AI help with exam schedules? +
Yes, Nexa AI can provide information about exam schedules if that data has been loaded into its knowledge base.
Does Nexa AI work 24/7? +
Yes, Nexa AI is available around the clock. For urgent administrative matters, we recommend contacting university staff directly during office hours.
Which campuses does IUB have? +
IUB operates multiple campuses including Baghdad-ul-Jadeed, Abbasia Campus, Khawaja Fareed Campus, and Rahim Yar Khan Campus.
What language does the chatbot support? +
Currently Nexa AI primarily supports English. Urdu language support may be added in future updates.
How does Nexa AI find answers? +
Nexa AI uses a RAG pipeline powered by LangChain and Pinecone to search a university knowledge base and generate accurate responses.
Is my chat data stored? +
Conversations may be logged for quality improvement purposes. No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties.
What if the bot gives a wrong answer? +
If you receive an incorrect answer, please contact the relevant university department directly. You can also report the issue to the development team.
How do I find my department's contact? +
Ask the chatbot something like "What is the contact number for the Computer Science department?" and it will retrieve the relevant details.
Can I ask about hostel facilities? +
Yes, ask the chatbot about hostel availability, fees, and contacts. IUB provides separate hostels for male and female students across its campuses.
What are the library timings? +
The Central Library is generally open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays. Ask the chatbot for the latest or specific campus library hours.
How can I apply to IUB? +
Applications are submitted through the IUB admissions portal. The chatbot can guide you to the correct URL and explain required documents.
Is there a fee structure available? +
Yes, you can ask the chatbot about semester fees for various programs. Official fee structures are also posted on the IUB website each academic year.
Can I get bus route details from the bot? +
Absolutely. Ask about specific stops like BJC, AC, KH, or FC and Nexa AI will return the relevant transport route and timing information.
Who built Nexa AI? +
Nexa AI was developed as a Final Year Project (FYP) by an IUB Computer Science student using LangChain, Pinecone, Flask, and OpenAI APIs.
